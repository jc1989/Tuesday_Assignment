Kenen & Kel

Are you afraid of the dark was a Canadian horror fantasy TV series that aired during the 1990s, and was played on Nickelodeon. The series premiered on Canadian T.V with the episode "The Tale of the Twisted Claw". Once the ratings reflected that it was a hit, Nickelodeon picked it up and it soon took off in the United States.
