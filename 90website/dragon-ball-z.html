Dragon Ball Z

Dragon Ball Z is a Japanese anime T.V series that is a sequal to Dragon Ball anime and is a part of the Dragon Ball manga series. Dragon Ball Z first aired in Japan on Fuji TV before being aired on American T.V networks. Dragon Ball Z followed the adventures of Goku and his family and friends as they fight against villains from all over the universe. The story follows the evolution of the characters and their progression as warriors who must constantly hone and practice their skills.
