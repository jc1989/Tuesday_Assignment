Boy Meets World

Boy Meets World was an American sitcom that aired on ABC networks during the 1990s. The story chronicled the events and life lessons of the main character: Cory Matthews. The plot of the show grew with the characters, introducing love interests, cultural changes, and the evolution of Corys life from middle school to college.
